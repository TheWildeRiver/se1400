Competition Summary

What is your competition doing right?

My competition is effectively showcasing their portfolios with clear organization and professional design. They provide detailed descriptions of their projects and highlight their skills and experiences in a way that is easy to understand.

What are they doing wrong?

Some competitors may have cluttered layouts or lack clear navigation, making it difficult for users to find the information they are looking for.

What could I do to prevent those problems?

I can focus on creating a clean and organized layout with simple navigation. I will ensure that each section of my portfolio is clearly defined and that users can easily access the information they need.
