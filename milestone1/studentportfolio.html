Web Summary

What content will the site contain?

The site will showcase my projects, skills, and experiences. It will include sections for my portfolio, resume, and contact information.

Where will lyou get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

I will create original content, including writing descriptions of my projects and experiences. I will also use images that I have created or have permission to use.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

Users should care because the site provides a comprehensive overview of my skills and experiences, which can be valuable for potential employers or collaborators.

Why should a site be built for this?

A website is a professional way to present my portfolio and make it easily accessible to a wider audience. It allows for better organization and presentation of my work compared to other formats.

What would ou like users to do at your site?

I would like users to explore my projects, learn about my skills and experiences, and potentially reach out to me for opportunities or collaborations.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal of the site is to effectively showcase my portfolio and professional background, making it easy for potential employers or collaborators to understand my capabilities and reach out to me.
